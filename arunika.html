Arunika

(Shri Arunkumar R. Mehta centre of learning with ultra-modern girls hostel for 120 students)

Smt. Vanmala Bahadurlal Jain Bhavan
Plot No. 36, Hingwala lane, Ghatkopar (East), Mumbai - 400 077 (Maharashtra), India

1500 sq. mt. plot bequested by Shri Nagindas Amulakhrai Mehta Gram Vikas trust, Mumbai

Associated Institutions

Behanshri Hiralaxmi, Smt. Kasumbaben and Shri Keshavlal Talakchand Sheth Institute for Women Development.

Smt. Sunilaben Kantilal V. Shah girl’s hostel.

IPCA academy of Health Science.



Infrastructure

12 story building with 60000 sq feet carpet area.

2 min walking distance from Ghatkopar Railway station (east) and metro station.

Safe and secured locality.

Main Activities

Ultra-Modern Girls hostel with capacity of 120 students.

Learning center for various courses in the fields of vocational, health, finance, management, law, gems & jewellery, insurance, UPSC, banking, mass media, computer science, engineering etc. This would be open for public, both boys & girls.

Benefits to Jain Community and general public

Assisting Jain Girls for their undergraduate and postgraduate education.

Giving education in various branches at affordable cost to general public.

Thus, Contributing for community and nation’s growth

The total cost estimated for this project is approximately Rs 75 Crores. (Land excluded)
As of date we have promises of about Rs 45 Crores
Donations are exempt under section 80G of Income tax and approved for CSR contributions
