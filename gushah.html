Shri Gambhirchand Umedchand Shah Jain Chhatralaya,
Anand (Gujarat)

With generous donation of Shri Gambhirchand Umedchand Shah parivar and others, Shri Gambhirchand Umedchand Shah Jain Chhatralaya, Anand was started in the year 2010.

We admit approx 100 college going students, provide comfortable lodging, boarding and free-ship to needy students.

Till date more than 1200 students have benefited for their education in commerce, science, law, medicine, engineering, CA, foreign languages etc and many have achieved glorious success in their lives

Facilities

Well infrastructured facilities
Well furnished rooms with necessary requirements
Lodging for 3/4 students on a sharing basis in hostel rooms
Mess for students (JAIN FOOD)
Geysers, water cooler, washing machine, gas stove
Library and/or reading room facilities
Sports and games room
Restricted & monitored WIFI / INTERNET facility for students
Giving loan scholarship (full or half) to the deserving students staying in hostels under "Loan Trust Scholar Scheme". Loans are repayable once student starts earning
